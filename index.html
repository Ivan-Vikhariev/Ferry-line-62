Hamburg's legendary ferry line 62

A classic for all Hamburg residents who want to show their guests the city: A trip on the HADAG ferries in the HVV network. The ferries on the Elbe are part of Hamburg's local public transport system and therefore the 49-euro ticket also applies here. You can make the most beautiful round trip with the ferries on line 62: They start at the Landungsbrücken and run via the fish market and the Elbe beach at Övelgönne to Finkenwerder and back. Passengers can get off at any of the stops along the way and simply take one of the next ferries. In sunny weather, the deck offers fantastic views of the harbour, Blankenese and the Elbphilharmonie concert hall. Those who prefer a more cosy atmosphere are well catered for in the passenger compartment

      Stations of the route:
    • Landungsbrücken
    • Altona (Fischmarkt)
    • Dockland (Fischereihafen)
    • Neumühlen/Övelgönne
    • Bubendey
    • Finkenwerder
