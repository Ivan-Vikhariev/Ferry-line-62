Bubendey-Ufer

The Bubendey-Ufer rear light, together with the Bubendey-Ufer front light, forms the Bubendey-Ufer leading light line for ships travelling up the Elbe in the Waltershof district of Hamburg. The tower is red and white striped and has a red lantern house. It stands around 1,010 metres away from the front light directly at the western entrance to the Parkhafen, for which it has no significance, however, and a new rear light was planned for 2013/14, as the previous rear and front lights had to be demolished due to the expansion of a terminal.
