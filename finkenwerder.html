Finkenwerder

Finkenwerder is located on the southern bank of the Lower Elbe and is bordered to the south by the Alte Süderelbe and to the east by the Köhlfleet. The communities to the south-west, beyond the Mühlenberger Loch, belong to the Altes Land. A considerable part of the peninsula is taken up by agricultural land and the Airbus factory site with the Hamburg-Finkenwerder airfield, which is also the headquarters of Airbus Operations GmbH. The district of Waltershof, which also belongs to the Hamburg-Mitte district, borders Finkenwerder to the east and is geographically separated from Finkenwerder by the Köhlfleet and Dradenauhafen harbour. Four neighbourhoods in the district of Harburg border Finkenwerder to the south: Altenwerder behind the Aue, Francop and Neuenfelde on the other side of the Alte Süderelbe and Cranz at the western end of the Mühlenberger Loch. There, Finkenwerder has a western border within the Elbe with the Lower Saxony municipality of Jork in the district of Stade. The districts of Blankenese, Nienstedten and Othmarschen on the other side of the Elbe in the district of Altona border Finkenwerder to the north.
